The Story of Calvin Kwok

My name is Calvin Kwok. I was born on the twenty-second of March in the year two-thousand-and-one. I went to elementary school in P.S.1 for Pre-K and Kindergarten, then transferred to P.S.173 for First Grade to Fifth Grade. I went to middle school in M.S.216, otherwise known as George J.
