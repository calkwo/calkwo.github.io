My Hobbies
Basketball Video Games Watching TV/Movies
In my freetime, I like to meet up with friends to go outside and play basketball. I either go to my elementary school's (P.S.173's) park or a recreation center. I typically go to my elementary school's park because it is near my house. However, I like to go to the farther park in the recreation center because it has an indoor basketball court and the floor is flat whereas P.S.173's basketball court is outdoors and the floor is slightly slanted. However, on days with bad weather, being unable to go outside, I am on my computer playing video games. I personally enjoy the way that video games are able to challenge my reflexes. Playing video games also allow me to live in a fantasy where I have magic powers. On bad weather days, contrary to popular belief, video games do get boring. When this happens, I am typically watching TV shows. Some of my favorites include Arrow, The Flash, Legends of Tomorrow, Supergirl, Iron Fist, and Agents of Shield (you can see that I love superheroes). Sometimes, I get on my computer and watch shows with my friends. My friends and I would both get on a call in Skype and then watch the TV show or movie together. Being able to talk with someone while watching a show or movie definitely makes the watching experience a lot more fun.
This is the recreation center that I enjoy playing at. It is the recreation center nearest to my house. At home, I use a Dell computer and I previously owned a Dell laptop. Netflix is a video streaming service which I use to watch movies/shows with friends.
I joined soccer club in elementary school and the only reason why I ended up switching over to basketball club was because soccer club was canceled after the coach injured his leg. Despite one of my friends moving to Florida, I'm still connected with him in that we frequently Skype and watch movies together.
